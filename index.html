About

Take a Glimpse at Taofiq Atoyebi's Background and Academic Leadership Experience

Taofiq Atoyebi is a dynamic and visionary business development expert with an impressive track record of driving growth and innovation across diverse industries. With a keen understanding of market trends and a sharp eye for detail, he has consistently demonstrated his ability to transform ideas into successful ventures. His journey is marked by a relentless pursuit of excellence and a passion for helping businesses reach their full potential.

From the outset of his career, Taofiq showcased his leadership abilities and commitment to making a difference. In 2011, he demonstrated exceptional leadership and negotiation skills by spearheading a crucial negotiation between the pre-degree students and the management of Adekunle Ajasin University. At that time, the university's policy only admitted candidates scoring 200 or above in their JAMB exams, leaving many students who scored between 180 and 200 without admission prospects. Despite scoring 216 himself, he did not stand by idly; instead, he led a team to the Vice Chancellor, successfully advocating for a policy change that benefited many students.

He later moved to Ọ̀sun State University, where he pursued a degree in Urban and Regional Planning. His leadership prowess continued to shine as he was elected the National President of the Urban and Regional Planning Students Association of Nigeria (URPSAN). Under his leadership, URPSAN became the first association of its kind in Africa, setting a precedent for student associations continent-wide.
